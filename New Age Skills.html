STRATEGY

Before you pick a track

Three ground rules that apply no matter which of the six builds below you choose.

Don't quit college for this
Keep the degree as your safety net and social network. Build the real skill in the hours outside class — evenings, weekends, semester breaks.
Learn by building, not by watching
Free YouTube first → paid bootcamp once you know what you don't know → internship the moment you have something to show. A public portfolio (GitHub, Behance, a personal site) beats a certificate every time.
Transition on proof, not hope
Don't leave a day job until side income has matched your primary income for three consecutive months. Momentum lies; three months of bank statements don't.
